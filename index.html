JOURNEY

Hi! I am Charryl Subrio, a 19-year-old tech enthusiast with a passion for innovation and a knack for untangling digital enigmas.

My journey in tech began at age 10, when I helped my older brother run our family's internet cafe. I learned how to build, upgrade, and troubleshoot PCs, as well as value customers and work under pressure.

In junior high, I enrolled in Information and Communication Technology (ICT), where I learned Ethernet cable termination and network architectures. In senior high, I took Computer Systems Servicing (CSS), which expanded my knowledge of networks and team leadership.

During my work immersion at Advance Monitoring & Security Systems, Inc. (AMSSI), I learned how to terminate coaxial cable, install CCTV, and set up DVR and NVR. I also won a wireless CCTV camera for my performance in their final practical exam.

After 1 month working as an intern, I was hired by AMSSI as a part-time CCTV installer. I also upgraded our family's internet cafe to a diskless setup, building the server, installing the NOS, and setting up the diskless system software remotely.

Currently, I am pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) at Holy Name University (HNU), where I am immersing myself in C language programming, HTML5, and CSS web development. At the same time, I am also taking courses online to further my skills, constantly seeking new challenges and opportunities to enhance my knowledge and skillset.

I am passionate about using technology to make a positive impact on the world. I believe that technology has the potential to solve some of the world's biggest problems, and I'm excited to be a part of that.

If you're looking for someone who is eager to learn and grow, I'm your person. I'm also a team player, and I'm always willing to help others succeed. Let's connect and see what we can accomplish together!
